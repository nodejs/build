### nodejs/build

* Potentially add libclang as a build dependency [#4350](https://github.com/nodejs/build/issues/4350)
    * We will look into details on how this will affect our supported platforms and will create sub-issues accordingly.
* Node.js 20 End-of-Life action items [#4306](https://github.com/nodejs/build/issues/4306)
    * FreeBSD machines have been taken offline.
* Update Jenkins to v2.555.1 [#4304](https://github.com/nodejs/build/issues/4304)
    * armv7 containers were done this week.
    * Looking into cross-compilers used for Node 22 right now.
* Upgrade to Clang 20 and Rust 1.88  [#4265](https://github.com/nodejs/build/issues/4265)
    * RHEL based platforms have been updated.
